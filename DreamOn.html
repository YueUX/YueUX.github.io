Dream: On

A Sleeping App
that you can breathe through

  Project Type: User Interface Redesign

  Role: UI Designer (Individual)

  Tool&Skills: Sketch, Visual Design

Background
                    

This is a project to redesign the interfaces of Dream:ON with a visual focus. Dream: ON is an iOS application that allows you to select what you want to dream about before you go to bed, monitors your movement during the night, then plays a themed soundscape at the optimum moment in your sleep cycle. The app experience before was poor due to outdated interface design and bad usability.
