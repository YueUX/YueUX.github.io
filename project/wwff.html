Will Work For Food

Community Impact Project | Website Redesign

——————————  \\\/// ——————————

This is a volunteering project where I dedicated to utilize my design and programming skill to give back to the community. Will Work For Food is a student-run non-profit organization, dedicated to raise money through participant's work to  fight child malnutrition abroad . I worked with WWFF to redesigned their website which serves as the main platform for the participants to raise and asked for donation. I learnt from the organization that the current website is somewhat "discarded" due to its poor usability, so there is an urgent need to redesigned it with clear information architecture, and well-tuned interfaces.
