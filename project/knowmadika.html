Client: Max
Current Logo: none
Client Product Brief: Knowmadika is a iphone application designed to facilitate content-rich nomadic inquiry-based learning and engagement for K-12 students in a museum. The client product incorporates “scaffolding features”, such as guiding prompts and tools, that aim to support and motivate students to mindfully collect, share and organize data and information in the museum while maintaining free-choice to visit exhibits that they find most interesting.

In this project, I created a branding package for the client's product by Adobe Illustrator, including logotype, styleguide,

1. LOGO DESIGN




The product "Knowmadika" comes from idea of open exploration, and how it relates to human interaction and teamwork, and the client proposed some logotypes they like. I took inspiration of the product value on"exploration", "openness" and "interaction", and set the visual style as "vivid while rigorous, fun while organized". 2. Logo Develop


To highlight the client goal of the product as being organized and scaffolded, I settled down on the second design, and further developed it, including its free space guide, scalabitility and color conversion. I also proposed three design of the Knowmadika T-shirt.

       
  Typeface - Federo

Federo is a typeface with variation of stoke widths. The use of this typeface
aims to create a clasisic and artistic feeling which resonates with the museum. At the same time, the dierent width of strokes adds a sense of vitality to the logo, thus makes it more suitable for the target user of k-12.

3. Website Mockups
