ABOUT THE PROJECT

I worked on this project during my internship at Digitas NYC Technology capability for our client Nissan Motor Acceptance Corporation (nissanfinance.com). The NMAC Account Manager app allows car buyers of Nissan and Infiniti to manage their car financing account online. The team and I helped diagnose and redesign the NMAC mobile app for a better user experience.

I performed the role of business analyst and collaborated with two senior UX designers throughout the duration of this project. My experience and skill set as a UX designer came as a strength to fulfill my duties and beyond to contribute to the design and delivery.
